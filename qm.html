What is Photography!?!
My definition of photography is an artform. It is a way of expressing emotion that is hidden. It allows people to express who they are through their photos.
These photos were taken for a color theme I decided to do for a visually pleasing feed on Instagram.

I chose to do a color theme because each color represents a different feeling, a different emotion. Colors are an amazing way of expressing feelings. Color is an artform.
